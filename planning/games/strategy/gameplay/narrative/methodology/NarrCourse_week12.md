## Introduction to Ren'Py
Ren’Py is a free visual novel engine that uses Python for scripting logic. It is designed for interactive storytelling, combining text, images, and sound to create branching narratives, visual dialogue, and player choices.
Official site: [renpy.org](https://www.renpy.org)

# Homework 

## Ren'Py Second Steps

### Assignment Overview
Create a short playable Ren’Py scene that expands upon the basic storytelling and branching dialogue we explored last week. You are welcome to revisit your week 11 homework or start with something new.

### Requirements
Your project must include:
- At least two different scenes or backgrounds
- At least one character sprite
- A minimum of five lines of dialogue
- At least one transition between scenes (for example: fade, dissolve, or similar)
- At least one background music track
- At least one sound effect (for example: footsteps, door opening, etc.)
- At least one variable that affects dialogue or scene progression
- At least one menu choice with at least two options leading to different outcomes
- A credits.txt file listing all artwork attributions (if needed)

### Guidelines
- Use original artwork/sounds or unlicensed/freely licensed media.
- The project should take no more than four hours to complete.
- Export your project folder as a .zip file and upload it to the Canvas assignment.
