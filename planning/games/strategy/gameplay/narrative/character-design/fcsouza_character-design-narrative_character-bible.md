# Character Bible Template

Fill out one copy per major character. Minor characters can use an abbreviated version (Identity + Gameplay Role only).

---

## Identity

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | _Full name, nicknames, aliases_ |
| **Role** | _Protagonist / Antagonist / Ally / Mentor / NPC / Boss_ |
| **Archetype** | _Hero / Trickster / Sage / Shadow / Herald / Threshold Guardian / etc._ |
| **Age** | _Age or apparent age_ |
| **Species/Race** | _If applicable to the game world_ |
| **Faction** | _Faction or group affiliation_ |
| **Title/Rank** | _Official title or rank within their faction_ |

## Personality

### Core Traits (pick 3-5)

1. _e.g., Determined_
2. _e.g., Sardonic_
3. _e.g., Loyal_

### Flaws (pick 2-3)

1. _e.g., Reckless_
2. _e.g., Distrustful of authority_

### Motivations

- **Primary Goal**: _What they want most_
- **Secondary Goal**: _What they want but would sacrifice for the primary_
- **Fear**: _What they avoid or dread_
- **Secret**: _What they hide from others_

### Values

- _What principles guide their decisions?_
- _What would they never do, even under pressure?_

## Character Arc

| Stage | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Starting State** | _Who they are at the beginning — beliefs, flaws, limitations_ |
| **Catalyst** | _What forces them to change_ |
| **Midpoint Shift** | _How they begin to change — what do they learn or lose?_ |
| **Crisis** | _The moment that tests their growth — do they regress or push forward?_ |
| **Resolution** | _Who they become — how have they changed? What did it cost?_ |

## Gameplay Role

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Function** | _Quest giver / Companion / Vendor / Boss / Guide / Obstacle_ |
| **Abilities** | _If a companion or boss, what are their gameplay mechanics?_ |
| **Player Interaction** | _How does the player interact with them? Dialogue, combat, trade?_ |
| **Availability** | _When and where the player encounters them_ |
| **Affinity System** | _Can the player build or lose reputation with them? How?_ |
